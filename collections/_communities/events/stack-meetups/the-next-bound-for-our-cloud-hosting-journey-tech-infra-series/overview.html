Overview

Government workloads are typically sensitive and unable to directly use commercial cloud hosting. Over the years, we have journeyed through different implementations of cloud hosting to benefit workloads in a secure and compliant manner. From each implementation, we attained certain successes and have learnt to do better.

This Meetup would cover key insights on the Government’s various implementations of cloud hosting including our next bound – implementing Government on Commercial Cloud + (GCC+). Agencies can learn how to gear for success on GCC+ and understand latest Cloud Service Provider (CSP) controls, such as the AWS Nitro System, to better secure their cloud journeys.

Who should attend: Tech professionals interested in the Government's cloud hosting journey; Levelling: Intermediate
